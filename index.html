About

Hi, I’m Bruno Giudice, a Video Editor & Motion Designer from Brazil, specializing in creating dynamic and engaging videos for YouTubers, brands, and content creators worldwide.

With over 3 years of experience using Adobe Premiere and After Effects, I bring stories to life with clean visuals, tight cuts, and animations that keep viewers hooked.

I believe editing is not just about cutting clips; it’s about transforming ideas into visual experiences that resonate. Whether it’s a YouTube video, a short documentary, or social media content, my goal is to help you capture attention and tell your story in the best way possible.
